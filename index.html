Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective immediately

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we collect your name and email address. We do not collect, store, or sell any other personal information. We use Google Analytics (gtag.js) to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, category clicks, and session duration — this data cannot identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To send you our daily sports and anime newsletter (only if you subscribe)
  • To analyze site traffic and improve user experience via Google Analytics
  • To display relevant advertising via Google AdSense
  • To respond to your inquiries when you contact us

3. Cookies & Local Storage

NexZone uses cookies and localStorage for: theme preference (dark/light), cookie consent status, poll voting, and PWA install dismissal. Google AdSense sets its own cookies for ad personalization. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can opt out of DART cookie usage by visiting the Google Ad Settings page. Third-party ad servers may also set cookies — we do not control these.

5. Third-Party APIs

We use ESPN's public API for live sports scores, Jikan (MyAnimeList) and AniList for anime metadata. These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data when our site loads content from them.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the EU or California, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion, and opt out of data sales. Since we do not sell personal data, the opt-out right is primarily about Google AdSense personalization (see Section 4).

7. Data Retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Contact form submissions are not stored on our servers — they are sent directly to our Google Apps Script backend.
